Accessibility Information

At A-W-T Adventure Park, we are committed to ensuring that all guests have an enjoyable and accessible experience. Below youâ€™ll find information about our accessibility options, including wheelchair-accessible facilities and the A-W-T Access Pass for guests with disabilities.

Wheelchair Accessible Rooms

We offer wheelchair-adapted hotel rooms for guests with mobility impairments. These rooms are not listed on our website, so please call us at 0800 or email us at A-W-Ts@attractiontkts.com to arrange your booking.

A-W-T Access Pass

The A-W-T Access Pass is available to guests with disabilities. You can apply for the pass either online, up to one month before your visit, or in person at the park. The pass provides priority access to rides and attractions to minimize wait times.

Accessible Facilities

Our park offers a variety of accessible facilities, including wheelchair-friendly paths, accessible restrooms, and designated seating areas for guests with disabilities at shows and events.
