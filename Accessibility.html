Accessibility Test

Do you sell adapted or easy-access A-W-T® hotel rooms for wheelchair users? Yes we do sell wheelchair adapted hotel rooms. These room types are not displayed on our website so please call us on 0800 or email A-W-Ts@attractiontkts.com and we can arrange this for you. I have a disability, how do I get the A-W-T® Access Pass? You can apply for the Access Pass once in the resort or on the A-W-T® website up to 1 month before your visit.
