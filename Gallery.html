The Thames Path is a long distance walking trail, following England’s best known river for 185.2miles (298 km) as it meanders from its source in the Cotswolds through several rural counties and on into the heart of London. SPA

With the lure of the city and the calm of the countryside, this hotel and spa offers lots in the way of leisure and relaxation. After a morning of shopping or a brisk walk through the our place site, head to the spa and health club where you can choose to work out, join a fitness class or swim off lunch in the pool. Adventurous Activities

This sensational spin on a classic thrill ride has guests catching maximum air delivering fun for families of all ages. This electrifying attraction is located in the popular Alpine Fest area in the center of our beautiful park. Brace yourself for whirling mayhem on this 360° shot of adrenaline. For a new experience, try putting your hands in the air as you catch maximum air and speed for complete Pandemonium. When you’re ready to take a breath, sit back and be hypnotized by the electrifying light package as Pandemonium’s lights follow all the twisting, twirling motions of the ride.
