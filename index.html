Welcome to A-W-T

Discover thrills, reconnect with nature, and create lasting memories. Welcome Adventurers!

At A-W-T, we believe that life is meant to be an epic adventure, and we invite you to embark on an unforgettable journey with us! Nestled in the heart of nature, our park is a haven for thrill-seekers, nature lovers, and families looking for a day of fun and excitement.

Discover the Thrills

Whether you’re a seasoned adventurer or a first-time explorer, our park has something for everyone. Dive into the excitement with our exhilarating zip lines, navigate the treetops on our challenging ropes courses, or test your courage on our towering climbing walls. Every corner of A-W-T offers a new thrill and a chance to create lasting memories.

Experience Nature

Beyond the adrenaline-pumping activities, A-W-T is a place to reconnect with nature. Wander through our scenic trails, marvel at the breathtaking views, and enjoy the serenity of our lush forests and sparkling streams. It’s the perfect backdrop for picnics, family gatherings, or simply unwinding in the great outdoors.

Fun for All Ages

Our adventure park is designed for adventurers of all ages. Little ones can enjoy our specially designed play areas, while teens and adults can take on more challenging courses and activities. We also offer guided tours and educational programs to learn about the local flora and fauna.

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. Our experienced staff is dedicated to providing a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors. We use state-of-the-art equipment and follow stringent safety protocols to ensure you can focus on the fun.

Join Us

We can’t wait to share the adventure with you. Explore our website to learn more about our attractions, plan your visit, and discover the magic of A-W-T. Whether you’re here for a day or a weekend, every moment at our park is an opportunity to make memories that will last a lifetime.

Animals Encounter

Learn more about animals and connect in an inspiring new way with the ocean’s most powerful predator. Discover the importance of play for both sea and land animals and dive deeper into their masterful hunting techniques. The show features spectacular behaviours and will leave you with a deeper understanding of these magnificent animals and will inspire you to care even more profoundly for the wonders of our natural world.

Bobcat

Experience the thrill of The Bobcat, a wooden family coaster and first-of-its-kind in Six Flags History. Combining classic charm with modern innovation, this coaster invites riders on an exciting journey the whole family will adore. Soar 55 feet into the air and race along at speeds approaching 40 miles per hour. With upgraded ride trains ensuring comfort and tighter turns for maximum excitement, The Bobcat is an adventure suitable for riders at least 42 inches tall. Embark on a one-of-a-kind ride, exclusive to our park!
